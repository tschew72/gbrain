Commercial items and project delivery items must be reported separately.

- Commercial items belong in the commercial report / tracker view.
- Project delivery items belong in the PMO / delivery report.
- Do not mix commercial follow-ups into the delivery status summary unless they have moved into active delivery after award / PO / kickoff.
