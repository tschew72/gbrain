# Bugcrowd Savant

## Overview
Bugcrowd is pivoting to an AI strategy for preemptive security with a product called "Savant". They position it as addressing the problem of AI-assisted bug bounty reports drowning security teams.

## Signal
- **Source**: Bugcrowd marketing email (securityexperts@bugcrowd.com)
- **Date**: 2026-06-16
- **Product**: Savant
- **Claim**: Security teams are shutting down bug bounty programs because AI-assisted reports are overwhelming them

## Competitive Relevance
- Competitor in the cybersecurity space, though focused on bug bounty vs. EVVO Labs' consulting/model
- Worth monitoring for pricing/feature positioning in AI-assisted security
