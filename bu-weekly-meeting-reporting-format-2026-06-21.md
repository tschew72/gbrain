# BU Weekly Meeting Reporting Format

## Decision
Vince formalized the weekly BU meeting as a structured operating review for pipeline, revenue contribution, and execution hygiene.

## Operating directives
- Each domain owner gets a maximum of 10 minutes to present:
  - plan for the week on lead and deal follow-up plus new opportunities
  - progress on leads and deals
  - problems and asks
- Edwin Tan must present from the operations perspective.
- Before the meeting, all leads and deals must be updated correctly in CRM.
- Weekly check-ins should be based on the leads and deals recorded in CRM.

## Domain ownership
- Ryan Villegas: Digital Solutions and Service Delivery
- Kailash Amin: VAPT/Red Teaming, SOC/NOC, and IT/CyberSec Consulting
- Rodolfo C Gonzalez: Solution architecture
- Edwin Tan: SOC/NOC operations perspective

## KPI model
- Total BU revenue is measured across all three owners.
- Ryan Villegas and Kailash Amin are accountable for revenue generated by deals under their care.
- Rodolfo C Gonzalez is credited for successful deals driven by solution design quality and response speed, to be marked through a new CRM field for solution architect contribution.
