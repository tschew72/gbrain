## Rule

PM status/reporting sweeps must read live Teams channel messages as part of the evidence set, not just local tracker files, email, or memory captures.

## Why

COSEM had an update in the `Client - Cosem CTM` Teams channel, which means the Teams surface can carry newer state than the project brief alone.

## Practical consequence

When checking a project, include the Teams channel listed in that project's `## Comms Surfaces` section before finalizing status or reporting that no updates exist.
