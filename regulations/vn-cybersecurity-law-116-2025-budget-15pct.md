# Vietnam Cybersecurity Law 116/2025/QH15 - 15% Cybersecurity Budget Rule

## Note

Draft guiding documents for the Law on Cybersecurity (Law No. 116/2025/QH15), effective 2026-07-01, specify that digital transformation programs, projects, and information system development must allocate at least 15% of the total budget to cybersecurity.

## Key points

- Mandatory compliance: agencies and organizations must allocate at least 15% of total investment during construction, establishment, and operation of information systems for cybersecurity.
- Objective: ensure security requirements are built in from design through deployment, with adequate resources, personnel, and technical solutions against cyber threats.
- Related documents: draft guiding documents and draft decree on cybersecurity protection for information systems, aligned with current system security standards.

## Caution

- Treat this as draft guidance until verified against the Ministry of Public Security portal or the National Legal Document Database Portal.
- Use the rule as a planning assumption for budgeting and proposal drafting, not as final legal advice.
