## Standing rule

When posting the PM report, always:

- use strict age-based ranking
- include Teams evidence
- keep blocked separate from stale
- keep commercial items out of the delivery report
- treat SAT and Phishing as an internal delivery project
- override file age with live file/Teams evidence before labeling something stale

## Purpose

This keeps delivery reporting clean, prevents commercial items from leaking into the PMO feed, and stops active threads from being mislabeled as stale.
