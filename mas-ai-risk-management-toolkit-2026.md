# MAS AI Risk Management Toolkit (March 20, 2026) + TPRM Overhaul

## MAS AI Risk Management Toolkit
- **Published:** March 20, 2026
- **Scope:** Traditional, generative, and agentic AI
- **Components:** Executive Handbook + Operationalisation Handbook
- **Posture:** AI governance = evolving audited process (not static)

## MAS TPRM Overhaul
- **"Third-party arrangement" redefined** to cover ALL formal service contracts including intragroup entities
- **Internal relationships = same risk assessment as external vendors**
- Supersedes prior outsourcing guidelines for FMCs
